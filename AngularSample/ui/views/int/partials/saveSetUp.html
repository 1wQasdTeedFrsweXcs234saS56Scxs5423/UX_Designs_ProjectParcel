Acknowledgement - A notification is sent once the order is loaded into the system for the first time.

Delivery Date Change - A notification is sent for the first planned delivery date and any subsequent changes to the planned delivery date.

Order Change (watch order) - A notification is sent for any change on the order including status changes, date changes and item changes. This selection may result in a high number of notifications.

Status Change:
Admin/Processing - The order for a product has been received by the supplier.
Production - The product on the order has been moved into the production cycle at the factory.
Consolidation - The product on the order is at the consolidation hub and has not shipped.
Shipped - The product on the order has been shipped
Delivered - The product on the order has been delivered and HP has received a POD (Proof of Delivery). Not all carriers send POD dates; if no POD is sent to HP the order will stay in Shipped status.
Invoiced - The product on the order has been invoiced.

Hold Categories
Order Entry - The order has an order entry hold.
Factory Fulfillment - The order has an factory fulfillment hold.
Shipping - The order has an shipping hold.
Billing - The order has an billing hold.
Fallout - The order has an fallout hold
